We are a membership based organisation. If you support the idea of community-run tech and alternatives to big tech, or want to stay in touch, please consider joining as a member.

Members:

- Pay an annual membership fee:
  - Solidarity $100
  - Full $40
  - Concession/Precarious $10
  - Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander $0
- Are invited to our AGM
- Will be invited to participate in major decisions about our direction and activities

To join, please email membership@merri-bek.tech . Include your name, which membership level you’d like to pay, and your contact info: email, and Matrix handle if you have one
