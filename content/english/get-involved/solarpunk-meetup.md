We host a monthly Solarpunk Meetup in Merri-bek, to collaborate with green technologists from across Naarm (Melbourne) and foster related projects. You can register [via Meetup](https://www.meetup.com/merri-bek-tech/) or connect with us by email to events@merri-bek.tech. All welcome!

In 2026, this will be held on the third Monday of each month:

- [18 February 2026](https://www.meetup.com/en-au/merri-bek-tech/events/313073832/)
- 18 March 2026
